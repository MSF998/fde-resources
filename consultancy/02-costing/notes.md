## Core Frameworks / Methods

- How margins erode
  - Scope Creep accepted without change order
  - Token costs exploding beyond estimates
  - Data quality discovered mid-build
  - Client side delays eating your buffer
  - Rework from undocumented requirements
  - Maintenance underpriced as a percentage

- Pricing Models
  - Fixed Price
    - Scope -> Effort -> Rate x Contingency= Price
    - FDE obsorbs scope creep.
    - Client gets certainity
    - Best for
      - Well scoped, well understood work
      - stable requirements
      - clients needs budget approval upfront
    - Watch out for
      - Scope creep
    - Margin
      - Medium
  - Time & Material
    - Actual hours x day rate = Invoice
    - Client bears scope creep
    - FDE has low uncertainity
    - Best for
      - Risk Obsorption
      - Know environment
      - unclear final scope
      - discovery-heavy work
      - research phases
    - Watch out for
      - Client hate open ended bills
    - Margin
      - Low-Medium
  - Outcome-Based
    - % of value delivered (cost saved / revenue generated)
    - Shared risk and shared upside
    - Best for
      - High value automation
      - Fraud detection
      - Revenue impact cases
      - Established track record required
    - Watch out
      - payment lags delivery
    - Margin
      - Highest potential
      - Highes risk if baseline is set incorrectly
