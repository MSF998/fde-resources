## Core Frameworks / Methods

### Before I speak to anyone analyze the following

- Who am I talking to
- What do they speak
- What does it mean when the speak
- Hence how should I talk to them
- What should I not say, what makes them angry

## Key Principles

### Stakeholder Type Matrix

#### Identify the below ASAP

1. Champion
   - Aligned person. Person who has been soled on the idea
2. Sponsor
   - The one who sign offs. Cheque
3. Blocker
   - gate keeper. Skeptical person
4. End-User
   - Knows edge cases.

### FDE is a mindset
