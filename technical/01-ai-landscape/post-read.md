## What the Material Covered
- In 2017 Ashish Vaswani and team published “Attention Is All You Need.” It introduced two ideas that cracked the scaling problem:
    - the attention mechanism
    - Transformer architecture (encoder + decoder)

- Layer
    - Artificial Intelligence: The umbrella term that does something smart
    - Machine learning: AI that learns from data
    - Deep Learning: ML powered by nueral networks with many layers. Handles messy unstructured dat like images and voice
    - Generative AI: Deep Learning that creates new content
    - Agentic AI: GenAI + tools. AI with a task and a tool 

- Limitation of LLMs
    - Hallucination: Model invents facts, citations etc. It sounds righg but it is not.
    - Knowledge cut off: Every LLM's memory freezes on the day its training stopped.

- Jargons
